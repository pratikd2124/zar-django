#----------- FAQs ------

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please refer to our Return Policy page for more details.

How do I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your order. You can also log into your account on our website to view your order status and tracking information.

Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Visit our Shipping Information page for more details.

How can I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support team:
- Email: support@zarluxury.com
- Phone: 1-800-123-4567
Support hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM (IST)
We strive to respond to inquiries within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you offer?
We accept payments via major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Payments are processed through secure gateways to ensure the safety of your information.

How do I complete a payment via PayLink sent in an email?
If you receive a PayLink from ZAR Luxury via email:

-Open the email from ZAR Luxury containing the PayLink.
-Click on the provided link, which will redirect you to a secure payment page.
-Enter your payment details (credit/debit card, UPI, or net banking).
-Review the details and complete the payment.
-Upon successful payment, you will receive a confirmation email with the receipt and next steps.

#----------- How to register ------
For Service Providers, Material Providers, and Home Owners
1. Visit the Become a Member page.
2. Select the appropriate account type (Home Owner, Material Provider, or Service Provider).
3. Fill in the form that appears after selecting your account type.
4. Our team will verify your profile and send a payment link to the provided email.
5. After completing the payment, you will receive a 6-digit code via email.
6. Go to the Signup with Passcode page and enter the code.
7. Once verified, you can set a password.
8. Your account is now ready to use!

For Community Users
1. Check your email for a passcode. If you don't have one, please contact your property or society secretary.
   (Note: Your society/apartment/building must be registered with us for access.)
2. Once the passcode is verified, you can create your profile.
3. Your account will now be active.
4. Note: Community users can enjoy free access.
